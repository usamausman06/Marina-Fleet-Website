We are a family owned and operated fishing vessel. The Phideaux fishes year round for offshore gamefish (tuna, dolphin, wahoo, marlin). Most of the year we are based out of Outer Banks Marina Marina. Please call Capt. Jamie at (252) 473-8051 for bookings.


          We generally leave the dock at five a.m., and return between four and six p.m. Offshore gamefish can be found from fifteen to sixty miles from the inlet, so running and fishing times will vary from day to day. The fish we expect to encounter are tuna, dolphin, wahoo, marlin and sailfish, with a chance to catch king mackerel, cobia, shark, striped bass and blue fish. The striped bass/bluefish trips are generally run between November and March, call for details.            DOWNLOAD

All you need to bring is what you need to eat and drink for the day, appropriate clothing.
Captain Jamie Reibel has been fishing out of Oregon Inlet for twenty years. He was the mate on the Phideaux from 1988 until 1995
