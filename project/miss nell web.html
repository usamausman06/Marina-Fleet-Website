‘Miss Nell’ is a 44″ Custom Carolina Sportfisher built by Captain Sheldon Midgette of Roanoke Island, NC. Capt. Midgette is recognized as one of the innovators of the world renowned Carolina style boat. This is a proven offshore vessel and features ample seating, enclosed bathroom, and safe and dependable CAT diesel power.

          Captain John Morse, is USCG licensed and skilled in off shore and inshore fishing on the waters of the Outer Banks. We are equipped to handle everything from Bluefish to Blue Marlin. First Mate Ron “Otis” Jones is on hand to help land the fish of your dreams.

Miss Nell is named for John’s grandmother, Nellie Russell Morse. Miss Nellie Morse was an Outer Banks native, and lived to age 102. This is our 3rd boat, named in her memory. A lucky charm!            DOWNLOAD
