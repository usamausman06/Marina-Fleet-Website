ABOUT

The largest charter boat fleet in Destin owned by the authentic fishermen who have made their living charter boat fishing in the Gulf of Mexico for over four generations, we have forty full-time charter boats who put Destin and fishing on the map. Call us today to schedule your fishing trip.

Our Vision



                         Marina Fleet Pte Ltd is committed to provide tug services which are of the highest standard in terms of quality, safety and customer satisfaction. The team in Marina Fleet shall endeavour to seek continuous improvement to provide top class services in the region.

Our Promise



Every staff member under the employment of Marina fleet Pte Ltd shall always adopt a “ Customer First ” approach and ensure that every customer is always a priority customer. Our fleet is monitored via a tracking system vehicle ensures that the most suitable tug is deploy in a timely and efficient manner.

Boat Slips



Outer Banks Marina offers charter and transient wet slips which can be leased separately or in combination with indoor storage. Keep your boat inside our facility during the hurricane and winter months. When your ready to go fishing, we will put your boat in its own slip for the summer.
